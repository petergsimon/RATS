This is the RATS

The Remotely Accessible Target System is the most advanced long range target scoring system. Using high speed sampling we are able to do away with the spotting scope, and bring in the RATS!

More Info

Chronograph

Detection of the speed of the bullet, measured at the target platform.

Scoring

Measurements of shots taken are immediately scored and shown to the shooter on the screen.

Wireless

All communication between the shooter and the platform uses RF, which can reach distances of over 300 meters.

Target Advance

A scroll of targets is held in the target platform, and a new target can be displayed at anytime.

Bullet Detection

Each shot is automatically detected, which triggers the location of where the shot landed.

Multi Player

Multiple shooters can compete on the same platform - all shots will be differentiated and scored separately.
